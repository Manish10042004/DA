Overview

The Mobile Sales Dashboard provides an interactive and visual overview of mobile product sales performance. It enables business users and decision-makers to monitor key metrics, identify sales trends, and track performance across regions, products, and customer segments.

Objectives

Gain insights into total sales, profit margins, and sales quantity.

Track regional and product-wise performance.

Identify top-performing products and sales representatives.

Analyze monthly and yearly trends to support data-driven decisions.

Key Features

Dynamic Visuals: Interactive charts, slicers, and KPIs for better exploration.

Performance Summary: Highlights of total revenue, total profit, and key trends.

Regional Insights: Map and bar charts showing sales by country, region, and city.

Product Analysis: Breakdown of sales by brand, category, and model.

Trend Analysis: Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons.

Profitability View: Visuals highlighting high-margin products and segments.
